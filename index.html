Our Teachers

Manuel

Manuel is known throughout London for his popular, fast-paced and fiery classes. He infuses his high energy and challenging teachings with yogic knowledge, banging beats, as well as his unique charisma, humour and gift of the gab! Based on the Ashtanga and Iyengar tradition, his classes include an emphasis on breath, a steady stream of verbal suggestions and strong physical adjustments. For open-minded students looking for a challenge, Manu’s classes will push you to take your practice further. Stewuruji applies the ancient teachings of yoga to the modern world to create a thought-provoking class. Manuel talks the whole sequence throughout the class. Asanas are not demonstrated.

Bea

Bea is an approved KRI Lead Kundalini yoga trainer, and is qualifed at Level 1, 2 and 3. She is a touring UK mantra recording artist and dancer, who loves to embodying the sacred through movement, melody and rhythm. As a musician she has recorded an eclectic mix of 9 music mantra albums available as downloads. Sharing her music, the timeless teachings of Kundalini + indigenous technology, she encourages others to dance + sing their joyful depths, incorporating ceremony to consciously awaken and become the instrument and true light of the soul.

Alicia

Alicia’s classes are a moving mediation, enriched by her compassion, kindness and wisdom. She worked as an independent artist, writing, performing and painting, before discovering yoga in the late 1990s. Having explored many styles including Iyengar, Ashtanga and Kundalini, she travelled to India where she trained as a Sivananda teacher with Yogi Siromani. Her popular and vigorous classes will challenge you but leave you feeling serene and fulfilled.
